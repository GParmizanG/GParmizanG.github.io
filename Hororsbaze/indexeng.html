'Spy Kids' reboot doesn't impress critics.

Spy Kids: Armageddon premieres exclusively on Netflix on September 22. Original series director Robert Rodriguez has revived the once-popular franchise 12 years after the last film. The release told a completely new story, and the events of the previous films do not apply to it. Reviewers from leading Western publications were not impressed by the film. On Rotten Tomatoes, the release rating is 47% among critics and 68% among ordinary viewers. In comparison with previous films, the result is not even the worst: the score for the fourth part is 23 and 37%, for the third - 45 and 25%, respectively. “Armageddon” is criticized for the lack of new ideas, for the low-quality computer graphics and the acting of children who turned out to be too small for the main roles.
